About Priya

Working in marketing has taught me the power of storytelling and connecting with people's emotions. When I discovered VShare, I was immediately drawn to the transparency and the ability to see exactly how my donations were making a difference in real people's lives.

As a mother of two, I'm particularly passionate about causes related to child nutrition and healthcare. I believe every child deserves a healthy start in life, and through my donations, I hope to contribute to creating a world where no child goes hungry or lacks access to basic healthcare.
