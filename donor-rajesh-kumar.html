About Rajesh

As a software engineer working in Mumbai's tech industry, I've been fortunate to have a stable career and income. I believe in giving back to society, especially in the field of education. Having grown up in a small town with limited educational resources, I understand the transformative power of quality education.

My donation philosophy is simple: every child deserves the opportunity to learn and grow. Through VShare, I've been able to support multiple NGOs working in education, healthcare, and community development. It's incredibly fulfilling to see the direct impact of our contributions.
