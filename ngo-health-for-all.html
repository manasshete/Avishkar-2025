About Health for All

Health for All has been at the forefront of providing accessible healthcare services to remote and underserved communities since 2012. Our mission is to ensure that quality healthcare is not a privilege but a fundamental right for everyone, regardless of their economic status or geographical location.

We operate mobile health clinics, telemedicine programs, and community health centers across rural Karnataka and Tamil Nadu. Our team of dedicated doctors, nurses, and health workers have provided medical care to over 75,000 patients and conducted health awareness programs in 200+ villages.
