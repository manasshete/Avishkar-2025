About Hope Foundation

Hope Foundation has been dedicated to transforming lives through education since 2015. We believe that every child deserves access to quality education, regardless of their economic background. Our mission is to bridge the educational gap in underserved communities by providing books, school supplies, scholarships, and learning resources.

Over the years, we have impacted over 5,000 children across 50 schools in rural Maharashtra. Our programs include digital literacy, teacher training, infrastructure development, and nutritional support to ensure holistic development of children.
