About Nourish Lives

Nourish Lives is committed to eliminating hunger and malnutrition in urban slums and rural communities. Founded in 2018, we have been working tirelessly to provide nutritious meals, food security programs, and nutrition education to vulnerable populations across India.

Our comprehensive approach includes daily meal programs, community kitchens, food distribution drives, and nutrition awareness campaigns. We have served over 2 million meals and reached more than 15,000 families through our various initiatives.
