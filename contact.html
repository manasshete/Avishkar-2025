About VShare

Connecting hearts, creating change

VShare is a revolutionary donation portal developed as a hackathon project with a mission to bridge the gap between generous donors and impactful NGOs. Our platform ensures that every donation reaches the right hands and creates meaningful change in communities worldwide.

Built with transparency and trust at its core, VShare provides a seamless experience for donors to discover verified NGOs, make secure donations, and track the real-world impact of their contributions.
