About Amit

As a business owner in Bangalore's thriving startup ecosystem, I've witnessed firsthand how access to resources can transform lives and communities. My journey with VShare began when I realized that successful businesses have a responsibility to give back to society and create positive change.

Healthcare accessibility is close to my heart because I believe it's a fundamental human right. Through strategic donations and partnerships with NGOs, I aim to bridge the healthcare gap in rural and underserved communities. My goal is to ensure that geographical location or economic status never becomes a barrier to receiving quality medical care.
